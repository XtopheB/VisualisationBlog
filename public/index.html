Why you should never use radar plots

Mar 11, 2017 by Xtophe Bontemps
A post from data.visualisation.free.fr What is radar plot? Radar plots or charts, also called Spider or Web charts are very popular tools for representing individuals scores of brands, firms, NBA players recorded or evaluated for different variables, different activities or dimensions. A radar plot is a sort parallel coordinates plot but using polar coordinates. The axis start from the center of the view and diverge from there to form equi-angular spoke.
